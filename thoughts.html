Consulting

Good recommendations need an operating path.

Research and analysis matter, but a useful recommendation also needs stakeholders, process ownership, constraints, and implementation steps. My strongest consulting work sits where market logic connects to practical execution.

Operations

Small process fixes compound when teams actually adopt them.

Templates, validation checks, digitised records, and clear SOPs are not glamorous, but they reduce rework and make daily coordination easier. Adoption improves when the system fits how people already work.

AI productivity

AI tools are most useful when paired with structured judgment.

I treat AI tools as accelerators for drafting, research, automation, and code assistance. The value comes from asking sharper questions, checking outputs, and turning results into a usable workflow.

Learning

Technical learning makes business work more precise.

Auditing CS50 and management consulting coursework helps me connect business analysis with better data, systems, and implementation thinking.
